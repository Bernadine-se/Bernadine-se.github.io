About Me

I'm an analytical and detail-oriented professional with over a decade of experience in forensic science and a biomedical technology background, now channeling my expertise into the exciting world of data analytics. My journey from the laboratory to data science has been driven by a passion for uncovering insights and solving complex problems through systematic investigation.

Currently, I'm applying Python to build predictive models, perform natural language processing, and create clear, impactful visualizations. My experience spans the full data lifecycle, from data cleaning and exploration to modeling and interpretation, always with the precision and methodical approach that my forensic background instilled in me. I'm particularly drawn to projects that require critical thinking and an investigative mindset, whether that's classifying medical data or analyzing sentiment patterns in text.

Beyond my technical skills, I bring a creative perspective to problem-solving. This creativity was recognized when I became a Top 10 finalist in the Vodacom Durban July Young Designer Award in 2021, demonstrating that innovation and analytical thinking can beautifully complement each other. I believe this unique combination of scientific discipline and creative insight allows me to approach data challenges from fresh angles and deliver solutions that are both technically sound and elegantly presented.

When I'm not working with data, I'm continuously learning new techniques and exploring how emerging technologies can be applied to real-world problems. I'm excited about the potential of data science to drive meaningful change and am always eager to tackle new challenges that push the boundaries of what's possible.
